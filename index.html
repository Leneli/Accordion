1 - What is visa registration?

Visa registration is a way for the Federal Migration Service (FMS) to control and monitor migration in Russia. Registration involves obtaining a stamp on your migration card, along with a registration slip showing when you have registered your visa.

For a substantial amount of travellers, registration can be a simple and straightforward process. Though a number of visitors, on occasion, will experience some challenges, particularly those who are travelling to and from various locations. With that said, you should ensure that you have your passport, migration card and registration slip with you at all times when leaving your accommodation, in the event that you are requested to present your registration details to the local authorities.
